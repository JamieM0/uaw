About This Tree

This tree represents the complete workflow for planning, building, and maintaining a theoretical Dyson sphere. Each node contains specific instructions and requirements for completing that step of the process.

The workflow is broken down into several main segments:

  • Planning Phase: Assessment of resources, design considerations, and energy needs
  • Construction Phase: Building the base, outer shell, and expansion of the structure
  • Energy Collection: Implementation of solar energy collection systems
  • Maintenance: Ongoing repairs, quality checks, and system maintenance
