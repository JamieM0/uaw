Our Mission

We aim to track automation progress, encourage its development, and provide a structured way for users to explore automation possibilities. By mapping the path to the technological singularity, we help make it more accessible and understandable.

Our collaborative approach brings together experts and enthusiasts to create a comprehensive resource for understanding what's possible with automation today and tomorrow.

How It Works

1

Task Breakdown

Our platform utilises Iterative AI to break down complex tasks into manageable steps, creating hierarchical trees of automation workflows.

2

Multiple Approaches

Each step can have multiple alternative breakdowns, allowing for diverse approaches to automation.

3

Community Feedback

Users provide feedback through questionnaires, helping us refine our automation trees and identify the most effective approaches.

4

Continuous Improvement

The system learns from user input, constantly evolving to provide better automation solutions.
