Intralogistics & Material Handling

Exploring automated movement, storage, and management of materials and goods within a facility.

Automation in intralogistics and material handling optimizes the flow of goods and materials within manufacturing plants and warehouses. This includes Automated Guided Vehicles (AGVs), Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs), automated storage and retrieval systems (AS/RS), and conveyor systems, all managed by sophisticated Warehouse Management Systems (WMS).
