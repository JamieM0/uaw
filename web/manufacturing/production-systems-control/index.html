Production Systems & Control

Exploring automation of overall production flow, scheduling, monitoring, and control systems (e.g., MES, SCADA).

Automation in production systems and control involves the use of integrated software and hardware to manage and optimize the entire manufacturing lifecycle. This includes Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) for real-time production tracking, Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) for process monitoring, and advanced planning and scheduling (APS) systems.
