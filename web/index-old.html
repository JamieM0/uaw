Our Mission

We aim to make complex automation processes tangible and testable through interactive simulation. Our platform transforms abstract workflow concepts into dynamic, validatable models that reveal genuine automation opportunities and identify real technology gaps.

By providing a simulation-first approach to process optimisation, we enable engineers, researchers, and innovators to experiment with automation scenarios before implementing them in the real world.

How It Works

1

Process Simulation Design

Design interactive automation workflows using our drag-and-drop editor with timeline-based task scheduling, resource allocation, and actor coordination.

2

Real-Time Validation

Simulations are validated against hundreds of metrics as you design, providing immediate feedback on structural integrity, resource conflicts, and economic feasibility.

3

Multi-Persona Analysis

Evaluate your simulation from different perspectives—hobbyist, researcher, investor, educator, or field expert—with customised validation criteria.

4

Export & Iterate

Save, share, and iteratively improve your simulations. Export validated models for implementation planning or further analysis.
